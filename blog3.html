DEFINING FUN TO DRIVE

FERRARI 296 GTB

The 296 GTB, an evolution of Ferrari’s mid-rear-engined two-seater sports berlinetta concept, represents a revolution for
the Maranello-based company as it introduces the new 120° V6 engine coupled with a plug-in (PHEV) electric motor capable
of delivering up to 830 cv. The car thus defines the idea of driving fun to provide pure excitement not only when pursuing
maximum performance but also in everyday driving.
