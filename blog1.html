THE ADRENALINE OF THE TRACK,
ON EVERY ROAD

The Ferrari 488 Pista is powered by the most powerful V8 engine in the Maranello marque’s history and is the company’s
special series sports car with the highest level yet of technological transfer from racing. In fact the name, meaning ‘track’ in
Italian, was chosen speciﬁcally to testify to Ferrari’s unparalleled heritage in motor sports. Technically, the Ferrari 488 Pista
encompasses all of the experience built up on the world’s circuits by the 488 Challenge and the 488 GTE.
